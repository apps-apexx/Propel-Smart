Our Story

Propel Smart

Christy and Megan are both military wives, with three kids and two dogs each. After working together in public education, they quickly realized that they also have similar educational philosophies and goals. In 2018, Propel Fairfax and Propel Loudoun were launched. Since then, the company has expanded to offer bicoastal in-person services and virtual services worldwide.
