Why Coaching?

Deliverables

Compassion

Having personal experience, we understand what it's like to struggle. This is a place to learn, grow, fail, and succeed without judgement.

Knowledge

During our sessions students will learn about themselves and gain strategies they can use when they feel stuck.

Confidence

The first step in every session is celebrating.  As students learn to appreciate what they do well and play to their strengths they will grow in confidence.

Accountability

Each week we will ask about the previous goals and action steps.  You'll have the opportunity to celebrate success and compassionately explore areas that were tough, then dig into ways to produce change.

Self-Awareness

Students take time during our sessions to understand their own actions, reactions, and thought processes. Becoming more self-aware allows students to figure out a way forward so they can make the desired changes in their lives.

Action Steps

Students choose how to move forward. Implementing 1-2 actions steps will allow them to slowly make some changes.

How does it work?

Coaching Session Structure

Celebrate and Plan

We start each meeting with a few minutes to discuss what went well in the past week.  Then we move into discussing upcoming plans and assignments.  Checking to see if there is anything due (or overdue) and deciding when there is time during the week to complete them. This gives students practice looking ahead and chunking up tasks.

Individualized Coaching

Next, students are asked to choose a topic for our session.*  We dive into a discussion of how the topic is effecting their lives and students discover strategies and plans to make their lives easier.

*Group Coaching is the exception - each class has a topic already assigned for each meeting.

Action Steps

In the last 5 minutes we will wrap up and discuss what the student discovered during the session and make a plan for the coming week. Each student leaves their session with action steps to implement in the coming week.

Individual Coaching

$50/session

1:1 coaching takes place over zoom.  Sessions can are generally 30-40 minutes but can be customized based on the student's needs.  The topics are determined by the student, we have tools to help the student come up with a topic if they aren't sure where to start. Sign up for a free Initial Meeting so we can get to know each other and decide if it will be a good fit to work together.

Group Coaching

$300/8 sessions

Group coaching consists of a zoom call with 3-5 students.  In group coaching each week we will work on a specific topic to learn strategies and make plans to improve in that area.  Students will practice that strategy during the following week.  We understand every student has different levels of comfort and some may not want to share in a group.  Students will have the ability to email and text to ask questions or share their success in addition to time on the call.
