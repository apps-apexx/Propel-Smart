Resources for College
  • Brainstorming:
    Talk to family and friends about your unique attributes.  Reflect on why you're interested in your potential major.
    Is there something in your life that has changed your perspective?
    What are your values?
  • Messy First Draft:
    The first draft should be completely imperfect - just write down your ideas and organize them later.  This keeps you from feeling stuck.
  • Editing:
    It takes several drafts to get your essay to the finish line.  Find 1 trusted person to give you honest feedback.
